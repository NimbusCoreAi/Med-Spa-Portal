## SECTION 3: MARKET VALIDATION DATA

### Market Size (Confirmed)
- **Businesses:** 10,488–42,622 med spas
- **TAM:** $384.7M (2025) → $1.47B (2035)
- **Growth:** 14.4% CAGR (fastest vertical)
- **Status:** ✅ Validated

### Willingness to Pay (Confirmed)
- **Current spend:** $150–$300/month per clinic
- **Premium segment:** $300–$500/month for HIPAA-compliant + integrated
- **Price sensitivity:** Low (high revenue per client)
- **Status:** ✅ Validated, even higher for compliance-focused solution

### Competitive Landscape (Documented)
- **Mindbody:** Dominant but not clinical; HIPAA gaps; declining quality
- **Zenoti:** Premium; too complex for small clinics; high cost
- **Vagaro:** Salon-first; lacking clinical depth
- **Niche players:** AestheticsPro, Pabau, Mangomint — purpose-built but limited distribution
- **Gap:** No incumbent owns "HIPAA-compliant + integrated clinical + booking + payments"

**Conclusion:** Wide-open market for specialized solution
