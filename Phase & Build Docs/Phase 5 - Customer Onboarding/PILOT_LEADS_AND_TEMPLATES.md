## Outreach Strategy

### Step 1: Research & Segmentation (Week 1)
- **Action:** Use the lead list above to segment clinics by:
  - Geographic region (start with 1-2 regions closest to you)
  - Owner type (physician, NP, entrepreneur, multi-location operator)
  - Size (boutique vs. multi-location)
  - Specialty (general aesthetic, laser-focused, dermatology-based, wellness)

- **Objective:** Identify your "warmest" 15-20 prospects (highest likelihood to respond + best fit)

### Step 2: Warm Outreach (Week 1-2)
- **Action:** If you have ANY mutual connections, start with warm introductions
- **Email:** Send 5-7 initial outreach emails from "warmest" list
- **Follow-up:** After 3 business days of no response, send follow-up email
- **Phone:** After email + follow-up, try calling directly if phone # is available

### Step 3: Expansion Outreach (Week 2-3)
- **Action:** Email next cohort of 10-15 clinics
- **Angle:** Use the pain-point template (double-bookings, intake completion, payment tracking)
- **Follow-up cadence:** Email → 3 days → Follow-up email → 3 days → Phone call

### Step 4: Selection & Closing (Week 3-4)
- **Meetings:** Conduct intro calls with interested clinics
- **Qualification:** Assess:
  - Do they have the pain points you're solving?
  - Are they willing to commit to 20-30 min calls every 2 weeks?
  - Can they start within 2 weeks of deployment?
- **Close:** Secure firm commitments from 3 clinics

### Key Success Metrics
- **Response Rate Target:** 15-20% (1 response per 5-7 emails)
- **Meeting Rate Target:** 40-50% of respondents agree to intro call
- **Close Rate Target:** 1 pilot per 3-4 intro calls = 3 pilots from ~15 intro calls
- **Timeline:** Complete all 3 pilot commitments in 2-3 weeks

### Important Note on Ownership Structure
In the USA, state regulations require medical spas to operate under the supervision of a licensed physician (the medical director), but they don't require the physician to own the business. In practice:
- Many med spas are owned by entrepreneurs, aestheticians, nurse practitioners, or investors
- The medical director provides clinical oversight but may not make business decisions
- When reaching out, try to identify the **owner/operator** (decision-maker) vs. just the medical director
